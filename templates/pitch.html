What it catches

The patterns you feel but can't name.

VibeLenz is trained on real fraud transcripts, romance scam cases, and manipulation research.

💬

Moving Too Fast

"I've never felt this way so quickly." Intense declarations of love or commitment before you've even met in person.

🚫

Always Has an Excuse Not to Meet

Every plan falls through. Too busy, traveling, camera broken. Someone who wants to date you will find a way to show up.

🎭

Story Doesn't Add Up

Details change. Claims to be a doctor, engineer, or military — overseas. Won't video call or always looks different.

🔒

Wants to Keep It Secret

"Don't tell your friends about us yet." Isolation from the people who would tell you something feels off.

💸

Financial Pressure

Starts small — a bill, an emergency, a favor. Escalates. Any ask for money from someone you haven't met is a red flag.

⏱️

Urgency and Pressure

"I need to know now." Deadlines and pressure to act fast are designed to stop you from thinking clearly.
